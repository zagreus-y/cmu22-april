    What is a Hackathon?

    A hackathon is an event, usually hosted by a tech company or organization, where programmers get together for a short period of time to collaborate on a project. The participants work rapidly and often work without sleep to achieve their task, as the events generally only last 24 hours or take place over a weekend.

    Why participate in a Hacktahon?

    Teamwork

    Hackathon teaches you how to work as a team and to be humble about letting go of your ideas once in a while.

    Leadership

    It’s the best place to learn, teach, challenge, test, and share everything! Ideas, technologies and methodologies. Hackathon is for those who believe that collaborating we can transform.

    Innovation

    During a hackathon, innovation is in the air! This is the time to use the latest technologies, learn new programming languages and, especially, take risks!

    Self-confidence

    Comming up with new ideas and implementing them in such a short amount of time gives immense satisfaction. At the end of the hackathon you know more than you knew before participating.
